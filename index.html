Founded in 2020, with a mission - to help others reach their full potential in science and technology, through interactive workshops and personalised mentoring. This is to increase the number of people entering the scientific and technical fields, whilst also helping to maintain the motivation to finish with success.

Our drive to help others comes from our own experience of working and studying science and technology, seeing how difficult it can be but also how incredibly rewarding. With the rapid development of technology, we also see the demand for educated people in these sectors is rising more and more every year. A demand that we would like to help by empowering those with an existing enthusiasm, as well as inspiring those who may not have previously considered an education in the technical fields.
